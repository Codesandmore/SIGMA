About The Event

Signal Processing Innovations and Growth through Mentorship Activities, SIGMA, conducted by IEEE SPS SBC MACE in collaboration with IEEE SPS Kerala Section, is the pinnacle event propelling signal processing innovation. Featuring dynamic workshops, mentorship, membership development sessions and expert-led talk sessions, SIGMA ignites passion and nurtures development in emerging technologies.
Welcome to SIGMA, where innovation ignites your potential.

Where

Mar Athanasius College of Engineering, Kothamangalam

F.A.Q

  • What do the different ticketing prices mean?

    The varied ticket charges for IEEE SPS members, IEEE members, and non-IEEE members at the event reflect the differential access to exclusive benefits.

  • Is there any benefits for early registration?

    Accommodation facilities might vary on a first come first serve basis

  • Where can I review my registration information?

    An email in regard to your registration will be sent to the email address that you have used for registration

  • Can I register and buy a ticket at the event?

    Spot registrations are not currently available

  • Is there preferred accommodation I can book?

    We could book hotels for delegates at their own risk by billing separately prior to the event

  • I booked at the preferred hotel. Are there any shuttles organized to transport me to the event?

    We'll arrange the transportation facilities in accordance to the accommodation that you prefer

  • Do I get participation certificates for SIGMA?

    Participation certificates worth KTU Activity points will be provided to the delegates only upon mandatory attendance on both days of the event.
